My-O-My | Brand Story

My style. My way.

“Luxury is an attitude. The only real luxury is time and effort, not about the price, more in defining your exclusive personality and the sense of style.” said by Ms. Charing, the founder and creative director of My-O-My. As a fashion jewelry brand from New York, to redefine the vision and mission of contemporary jewelry, My-O-My begins with “silver”, a pure metal material which is precious and mysterious.

New York is one of the fashion capitals which has the major influence on international fashion trends. Having the most exciting cultural and avant-garde identity in art, this city has also been noted that a strong profile and lifestyle with freedom and inclusive. Everyone can fully utilize their ample freedom to interpret their own uniqueness and individual characters. My-O-My deeply affected and inspired by this cosmopolitan city of vibrancy and freedom. By that, Charing established My-O-My with several like-minded friends with heart in fashion, design, art and music in New York. It truly reflects the contemporary spirit of solipsistic, and completely engaging aesthetic fashion brand of silverware.

Human values, beneficial and individuality are the core value of My-O-My’s product and service, which reflects the characteristic of co-founders. To meet people’s individual needs, My-O-My is confident, dynamic and fun, showing its extraordinary personality and temperament from the staffs to the store design.

With great passion and sincerity, My-O-My crafted a series of silver jewelry collection in both rustic silver, semi precious stones & diamonds including earrings, rings, necklaces, bracelets, pendants and other accessories. My-O-My jewelry uses high quality material, combining advanced technology and exquisite craftsmanship, and proposed in different cultures and artistic style.

Its forward-looking design with classic and chic elements is easy to match with any styles, which become to the epoch-making silver masterpiece.

There are eight series of My-O-My silver collection for the sake of meeting different personality and preference of styles, namely The Crown series in stylish and elegant, The Skull series in rock style, The Cross series with exquisite and ornate, The Shield series in chic extravagance, The Rose, The Star, The Knight and Love. All brilliant unprecedented silverware are special to friends with “My style. My way”.
